ABOUT US

Anax Lifescience, is a pharmaceutical company establised in 2007, Commited to improving the quality of the life of people suffering from chronic diseases.

For this purpose Anax is engaged in the marketing and sale of pharmaceutical products across the India.

Anax is highly professional and performance driven organisation that aims to provide the highest quality heathcare products for doctors and patient, whilst maintaining a high ethical standard in business operation.

We manufactured our products at USFDA, WHO-GMP certified facilities to provide best quality products.

The major segmentz s are NEUROLOGY, CARDIO-DIABETIC, GYNEC and ORTHO. We believe in providing innovative solutions to healthcare problems and possess an entrepreneurial culture to build divisions and products for the same. We retain a specialty marketing process in the indian pharma industry through our structure of customer-dedicated marketing teams and comprehensive product portfolios.
